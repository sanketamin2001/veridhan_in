About Veridhan

We exist to make honest investment advice accessible to everyone.

India has over 122 million registered investors, but fewer than 1,000 SEBI-registered Investment Advisers. Most retail investors still rely on commission-driven advice, social media tips, or expensive products that don't align with their goals. We are building Veridhan to change that.

What makes us different

Three things we refuse to compromise on

Zero conflicts of interest

We operate on a fee-only model. We do not earn commissions from mutual funds, insurance products, or any financial instrument we recommend. Our only revenue comes from advisory fees paid directly by clients.

Technology that serves humans

Our AI Research Engine automates data processing, scoring, and report generation — but every insight is reviewed by a human analyst. We use interpretable models specifically so we can explain our reasoning, which is also a SEBI requirement.

Built for compliance

We are not waiting for regulators to catch up. We are building within the SEBI framework from day one — explainable models, audit-ready reports, and full transparency in our methodology.

How we work

Principles we actually follow

  • Research first. Every recommendation is backed by documented analysis and a clear thesis. We show our work.
  • No conflicts. No commissions. No product sales. The only thing we sell is advice, and it is priced transparently.
  • Transparency. We communicate clearly about our processes, assumptions, and limitations. If a model has uncertainty, we say so.
  • Long-term thinking. We optimize for sustainable outcomes. We do not chase quarterly performance or short-term hype.
  • Technology as a tool. AI augments our analysts — it does not replace them. Every output goes through human review before reaching a client.
