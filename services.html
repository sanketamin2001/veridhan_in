What we are building for you

Everything below is designed around one idea: your advisor should have no reason to recommend anything other than what is best for you. Here is what that looks like in practice.

Core Service

Investment Advisory

Personalized, conflict-free investment guidance aligned to your goals and risk profile.

We charge a fixed fee — no commissions, no percentage of assets under advice. That means our incentive is always the same as yours: to make sure your money is working as hard as it can for your specific situation.

  • Portfolio construction and asset allocation tailored to your life stage and goals
  • Equity selection backed by documented research and a clear investment thesis
  • Risk assessment and ongoing risk management
  • Regular portfolio reviews and rebalancing as markets and your needs evolve
  • AI-assisted monitoring for portfolio-relevant events and opportunities
Research

Equity Research Reports

Independent, structured analysis for investors who want depth — not tips.

Our research reports go beyond surface-level takes. Each report includes a detailed analysis of fundamentals, competitive positioning, valuation, and risk — with a clear buy, hold, or sell thesis that explains our reasoning.

  • Company and sector analysis with financial deep-dives
  • Clear investment thesis for every recommendation
  • Macro and thematic insights for portfolio context
  • Event-driven notes on earnings, regulatory changes, and market-moving developments
  • AI-powered quantitative screening to surface opportunities human analysts might miss
Technology

AI Research Engine

For investors who want to run their own analysis with professional-grade tools.

The Veridhan AI Research Engine processes raw financial data — earnings filings, quarterly results, news, alternative data — and generates structured insights that would take a human analyst hours to produce manually. It does not make decisions for you; it gives you better information to make your own.

  • Automated processing of financial statements, earnings calls, and regulatory filings
  • Machine learning-based screening across custom criteria
  • Real-time sentiment tracking from news and market data
  • Watchlist alerts for material events and price-relevant developments
  • Customized research delivery based on your investment style

AI tools will only be deployed after SEBI registration and full regulatory approval. All outputs are subject to human review.
